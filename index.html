Privacy Policy / Gizlilik Politikası

Last updated / Son güncelleme: April 22, 2026

Electricity Tycoon is developed by Muniverse Studio. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Electricity Tycoon, Muniverse Studio tarafından geliştirilmektedir. Bu politika hangi verilerin toplandığını, nasıl kullanıldığını ve nasıl korunduğunu açıklar.

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent settings.

Uygulamamız reklam göstermek için Google AdMob kullanır. AdMob, onay ayarlarınıza bağlı olarak kişiselleştirilmiş veya kişiselleştirilmemiş reklam sunmak için veri toplayabilir.

3. Analytics / Analitik

We collect anonymous gameplay events to improve game balance. These events do not contain personal information:

  • Chapter progression milestones
  • Equipment & upgrade purchases
  • Ad watched events (type only, no personal data)
  • Daily game snapshots (economy stats, anonymous)

4. Third-Party Services / Üçüncü Taraf Hizmetleri

  • Firebase Authentication — anonymous & Google sign-in (Firebase Privacy)
  • Firebase Firestore — cloud save storage
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous gameplay analytics
  • Google AdMob — in-app advertising (Google Privacy)

We do not sell user data to third parties.

Kullanıcı verilerini üçüncü taraflara satmıyoruz.

5. Children's Privacy / Çocukların Gizliliği

The app is rated for ages 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The anonymous authentication system does not require personal information.

6. Data Security / Veri Güvenliği

All data transmitted between the app and Firebase servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Local game data stored on the device is not accessible to other apps.

7. Data Retention / Veri Saklama

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In-app: Settings → Delete My Account
  • Web: Account Deletion Page
  • Email: muniversestudio@gmail.com

8. Your Rights / Haklarınız

  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Use the app without a Google account (anonymous mode)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (GDPR / consent prompt on first launch)

9. Changes / Değişiklikler

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
